The Saga of Steve

A Legend of Zelda-like game implemented on the RP2040

Tangia Sun (tts47), Emily Speckhals (ems395), Max Klugherz (mck65)

The Legend of Zelda is action and adventure video game, published by Nintendo, that originated in Japan on the Family Computer Disk System in 1986. Now, there are over 20 subsequent official versions of the game. This project is our attempt to recreate the original to see what challenges the original gamemakers faced, while still taking our own creative liberties. Our design includes the use of the Raspberry Pi Pico as the core processing unit, as well as the use of a VGA screen for the display, a keyboard for user input, and sound ouput through a set of desktop speakers. While our implementation is not perfectly true to the original, it undoubtedly shows our commitment to a clean, working embedded video game.
